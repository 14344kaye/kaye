💖 Happy 1st Anniversary 💖

To my dearest love, Julius,

Thank you for always taking time to update me and talk to me kahit na busy ka at marami kang ginagawa. Sana mas dalasan mo pa pag update hehehe. Sorry sa pangungulit at pagtatampo minsan kasi miss na miss lang kasi kita🥹.

Salamat sa lahat ng pag-unawa, sa lahat ng effort na makakapagpasaya sa akin. I see you and your efforts to make me happy, my love. Marami akong ipinagpapasalamat sa Diyos dahil binigay ka niya sa akin. Binigyan niya ako ng bagong inspirasyon upang magpatuloy at wag sumuko sa buhay. I had a little tiny prayer on the back of my mind before, “Lord, I’m ready” and then you came, not in an unexpected way but you came in my life with ease, not forced, and not dramatic, but you came in just right. ♥️

There are days that I am in my low moments, sometimes overthinking and overwhelmed, but I am grateful and truly thankful that God made you a patient man, so patient that you do not fail to appease me, soothe me, and make me feel good, that I can’t help but crawl back into your arms every single time. I know that I may be too much sometimes but always remember that no matter how grumpy or jealous I get, I will always always always love you. 💘

My love for you is deeper than any negative emotions I have. I’m sorry for making things quite hard for you but know that I will always be by your side, supporting you, cheering for you, praying for you, and loving you with all my heart.💘

May we create more happy memories together and more moments to treasure forever. Happy 1st Anniversary, my lolove, my pogi, my baby, my lovie hubby, my love! 💗

Your lolove,
Niña Kaye

Sorry love, I tried to share my thoughts and make this letter in tagalog or hiligaynon but it’s easier for me to pour all my thoughts in English😅.
